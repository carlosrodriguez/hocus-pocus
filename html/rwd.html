Choose your preferred way of writing RWD by setting the $rwd-type variable to mobile-first or desktop-first.

Define custom breakpoints as $rwd-map variable. You can provide values with one or two sizes.

Each breakpoint gets visibility and grid helpers.

$rwd-type: desktop-first;
$rwd-map: (mobile: 680px, tablet: 681px 1200px);

<div class="mobile-hidden">...</div>
<div class="hidden mobile-visible">...</div>

<div class="grid">
  <div class="grid-item 1/4 mobile-hidden">
    ...
  </div>

  <div class="grid-item 3/4 mobile-1/1">
    ...
  </div>
</div>
