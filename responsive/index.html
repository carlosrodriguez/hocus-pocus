First you can define preffered way of writting RWD by setting the $responsive-type variable with min or max keyword. Then, you should define brakepoints in $responsive-map variable. There are two types of them: with single and double arguments. For each breakpoint will be generated visibility helpers and grid i.e.

$responsive-type: max
$responsive-map: (mobile: 680px)

<div class="mobile-hidden">...</div>
<div class="hidden mobile-visible">...</div>

<div class="grid">
  <div class="grid-item 1/4 mobile-hidden">
    ...
  </div>

  <div class="grid-item 3/4 mobile-1/1">
    ...
  </div>
</div>
